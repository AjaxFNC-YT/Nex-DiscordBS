# Nex Discord Bot - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 15, 2025

## 1. Legal Basis & Age Requirement
By using Nex, you confirm that you are **13 years of age or older** and legally able to consent to data processing under applicable laws.

## 2. Overview

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how Nex handles data when you use the Bot.

## 2. Information Collected
Nex may temporarily process:
- Epic Games authorization tokens
- Discord user identifiers
- Fortnite-related account data required to perform requested actions

## 3. How Information Is Used
- To perform actions you explicitly request
- To enable optional automated features you enroll in
- To verify premium status

## 4. Data Storage & Retention
- Nex does **not store account data long-term**.
- Logging out removes all account-related data.
- Premium status may remain stored unless a full deletion is requested.

## 5. Automated Features
If you enable automated features, Nex will only perform actions within the scope you approved. You may disable these features at any time.

## 6. Data Sharing
- Nex does **not sell, trade, or share user data** with third parties.
- Data is only communicated with Epic Games services as required for functionality.

## 7. User Control & Deletion Requests
You may:
- Log out at any time
- Request full data deletion, including premium status

Deletion requests can be made through our support server:

https://discord.gg/nNsJmKBM4x
